By Road

This beautiful hill station is well connected to the towns like Koppa, Sringeri; Kadur & Birur by road. There are two National Highways that pass through the district of Chikmagalur. Belur is 25 kilometers, Mudigere is 29 kilometers and Arehalli is almost 41 kilometers far from Chikmagalur.
